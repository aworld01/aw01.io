Battery

There are two types of battery

1. Lead Acid Battery
2. Tubular Battery
4. Dry Cell Battery
3. Gel Battery

C10, C20 Battery Calculation

1. 150AH - C10 150/10 = 15AH Charging / Discharging Current
2. 150AH - C20 150/20 = 7.5AH Charging / Discharging Current

Simple Battery vs Tubular Battery

Simple Battery:-
Life: 2.5 - 3 years
Plate size: 1 - 2 mm thick

Tubular Battery:-
Life: 4 - 6 years
Plate size: 3 - 5 mm thick
