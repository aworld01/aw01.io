Gold

There are two types of Gold

  • KDM Gold (Cadmium)
  • BIS Gold (Bureau of Indian Standards)

Cadmium contains toxic fumes harmful to the skin and Baned on Jan/2020

24 carat = 99.9% pure: (It's too soft to be converted into any jewelry and only Bars / Coins can be made.)

22 carat = 91.6% pure

18 carat = 75% pure: (mostly used for diamond jewelry as it helps to hold diamond)

14 carat = 58.5% pure: (made for special request)
