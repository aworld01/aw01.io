Blender

Spacebar: Play/Pause

TIMELINE

k: Cut
x / Delete: Delete
Ctrl+Z: Undo.
Home: Fit video footage on timeline.
Ctrl+End: Fit frames with video footage.
Ctrl+Scroll down: Move timeline from right to left.
Ctrl+Scroll up: Move timeline from left to right.
select videos+hold Left Click and move anywhere: Move freely.

PLAYHEAD

pg up: Move playhead at the end of video footage.
pg dn: Move playhead at the first of video footage.
Alt+pg up: Move right and goto center at video footage.
Alt+pg down: Move left and goto center at video footage.
Shift+Left Arrow: Move playhead at first frame.
Shift+Right Arrow: Move playhead at last frame.
Select videos, Shift+S: Set video at first frame.
