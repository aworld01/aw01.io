Blender 3.0.1

You need three button mouse to use Blender.

Basic

Toolbar: T Transform: N Delete: Select object, X, D. / Delete 3D cursor: Shift+RightClick Add: Shift+A. Move cursor to center point: Shift+S, Cursor to World Origin(1) Goto focal_point: Del Undo: Ctrl+Z Redo: Ctrl+Shift+Z Duplicate: Shift+D Goto home: Home Repeat: Shift+R

Viewport

Artho mode (2D) Perspective mode (3D) Rotate view: Push middle button and Hover. Move view: Shift+Push middle button and hover. Zoom in: (CTRL++) Scroll mouse wheel to computer screen side. Zoom out: (CTRL+-) Scroll mouse wheel to your side. Zoom in, Zoom out: CTRL+Puse middle button and Hover Foreward and Back.

SIDES

Front side: 1 Back side: CTRL+1 Right side: 3 Left side: CTRL+3 Top side: 7 Bottom side: 9


SPIN

Spin ACW: 4 Spin CW: 6 Spin bottom to top: 2 Spin top to bottom: 8


SELECT (Sift+Space, W)

Select one by one: (Shift+Click) Select all: A. Deselect all: dubble A. Deselect all: Alt+A Select by drag= B, Click+Drag Deselect by drag= B, Click_middle_button+Drag Select by drag: C, click and drag (Middle_click and drag to Deselect) (Shift+click and drag to Deselect) (Right_click to remove circle) (Esc to remove circle)

Free move: Select, G, Hover+Click X accesses move: Select, G, X, Hover, Click Y accesses move: Select, G, Y, Hover, Click Z accesses move: Select, G, Z, Hover, Click

Free rotate: Select, R, Hover+Click X accesses rotate: Select, R, X, Hover, Click Y accesses rotate: Select, R, Y, Hover, Click Z accesses rotate: Select, R, Z, Hover, Click Make normal: Select, Alt+R

SCALE (Sift+Space, S)

Free scale: Select, S, Hover+Click X accesses scale: Select, S, X, Hover, Click Y accesses scale: Select, S, Y, Hover, Click Z accesses scale: Select, S, Z, Hover, Click 2times bigger: Select, S, 2, Enter half: Select, S, .5, Enter Double scale X accesses: Select, S, X, 2, Enter Make normal: Select, Alt+S
